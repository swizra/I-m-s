Shipping Information

Review how KEFE prepares orders, handles dispatch timing, estimates delivery windows, and supports address updates or shipping-related issues.

Dispatch cut-off

Orders placed before 14:00 CET on business days usually enter processing the same day.

Primary support channel

orders@kefe.ch

Order preparation

Orders are prepared after checkout confirmation, payment review, and stock validation. Processing time may differ when inventory is low or an item requires an additional verification step.

Estimated delivery windows

  • Switzerland and Liechtenstein: 1 to 3 business days after dispatch.
  • Neighboring EU destinations: 3 to 6 business days after dispatch.
  • Rural destinations or holiday periods may require additional transit time.

Shipping updates and notifications

Customers can review order progress in the account area. Shipment notifications are shared as the order moves through processing, dispatch, and final delivery.

Address changes

Please review your shipping address carefully at checkout. Incorrect or incomplete delivery details can delay dispatch, create routing issues, or require manual customer support follow-up. If you need a correction, contact support immediately after ordering.

Split shipments and stock checks

KEFE aims to ship complete orders whenever possible. If stock validation reveals an issue with one item, support may contact you with replacement, delay, or partial shipment options depending on the situation.

Shipping questions

For delivery timing questions, lost parcels, or courier-related issues, contact orders@kefe.ch with your order number and the latest tracking status shown in your account.
