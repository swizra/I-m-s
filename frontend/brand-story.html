The KEFE Story

KEFE was shaped as a calm, modern self-care storefront where browsing feels approachable, product information stays useful, and every page helps customers make decisions with less effort.

Core idea

Pair a polished shopping experience with simple, understandable product discovery.

Project context

Storefront and inventory experience created for FHNW Internet Technology.

Why KEFE exists

The project is built to show how a compact brand experience can still support real shopping flows, customer accounts, order history, and admin-side inventory work.

What the brand stands for

KEFE is built around the idea that self-care shopping should feel calm, intentional, and easy to understand. Instead of overwhelming customers with noise, the brand emphasizes clarity, comfort, and quiet confidence.

What shapes the experience

  • Focused navigation instead of overloaded menus.
  • Product pages that prioritize quick understanding.
  • Operational pages that keep the management side visible and believable.

How the assortment is curated

Products are presented as part of a coherent self-care assortment rather than an unstructured catalog. Categories, descriptions, and featured collections work together to guide customers from intent to checkout.

Where it goes next

Brand pages like this can support richer storytelling around collections, values, and curated shopping paths without making the storefront harder to use.
