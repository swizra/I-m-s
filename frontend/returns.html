Returns and Exchanges

Review the KEFE return window, exchange process, refund timing, and the quickest path for damaged, incorrect, or incomplete orders.

Standard return window

30 days from delivery for unopened items in resellable condition.

Report damaged items

Within 7 days of delivery, with photos where possible.

What can be returned

Unopened items can generally be returned within 30 days of delivery if they remain in clean, resellable condition and include the original packaging. We review every request individually to confirm item condition and order history before approving a return.

Items that may not be eligible

  • Opened or materially used products unless they arrived damaged or incorrect.
  • Items returned without enough order information to verify the purchase.
  • Products that were altered after delivery.

Damaged, incorrect, or incomplete orders

If an order arrives damaged, contains the wrong product, or is missing an item, please contact support within 7 days of delivery. Include your order number, product name, and photos of the package or item so we can review the issue quickly.

How to start a return

Email orders@kefe.ch with your order number, the item you want to return, and the reason for the request. Once approved, you will receive the next steps for the return shipment or exchange review.

Refund timing

Refund timing depends on the payment method and the outcome of the review. Once a refund is approved, the visible processing window is usually determined by the payment provider rather than the storefront interface itself.

Exchanges

Exchanges are handled on a case-by-case basis depending on stock availability. If the requested replacement is unavailable, support may offer a refund or alternative item after reviewing the request.
