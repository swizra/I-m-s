Cookies and Browser Storage

KEFE uses cookies and browser-side storage to keep essential storefront functions working smoothly, including account continuity, cart state, and interface preferences across visits.

Main purpose

Support sign-in, cart continuity, session integrity, and repeat-use convenience.

Related document

Privacy Policy

Essential storage

  • Cart selections and item counts.
  • Customer sign-in state and session helpers.
  • Security-related data used to maintain trusted access.

Preference storage

KEFE may store basic interface preferences that improve repeat visits, such as display choices or page-state helpers that make the storefront easier to use on subsequent sessions.

Why it matters

Without basic client-side storage, some storefront features would need to be rebuilt on every page load, which can interrupt cart usage, account continuity, and other customer actions.

Analytics and diagnostics

KEFE may process technical diagnostics and operational data to monitor service health and troubleshoot issues. Where analytics features are enabled, they are used to understand store performance and improve the customer experience.

Managing storage

You can usually clear cookies and local storage in your browser settings. Doing so may sign you out, empty saved cart state, or remove recent interface preferences.
