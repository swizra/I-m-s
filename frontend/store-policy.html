Store Policy

This page summarizes the core policies that shape the KEFE storefront, including product availability, order review, promotions, account use, and site updates.

Scope

This page complements the Terms, Privacy, Shipping, and Returns pages.

Last updated

March 24, 2026

Catalog accuracy

KEFE aims to keep product names, descriptions, prices, images, and availability accurate, but details may change when inventory or product data is updated in the admin area.

Order review and fulfillment

  • Orders can be reviewed for stock availability, billing clarity, or data consistency.
  • Status updates may change as fulfillment progresses.
  • Some orders may be adjusted or cancelled if an item is unavailable.

Pricing and promotions

Prices, bundles, featured collections, and promotional messaging may change without prior notice. Discounts apply only during the stated campaign period and cannot be applied retroactively once an order has already been confirmed.

Customer reviews and feedback

Reviews and product feedback must be relevant, respectful, and based on actual customer experience. KEFE may remove content that is abusive, misleading, or unrelated to the product or service experience.

Fair use of the service

Customers may not use automated tools, abusive ordering patterns, or repeated false claims in a way that disrupts service quality for other shoppers. Where necessary, KEFE may limit, cancel, or review suspicious activity.

Site changes and maintenance

KEFE may update copy, collection pages, fulfillment rules, and administrative workflows over time. Planned maintenance or feature changes may briefly affect storefront availability or account access.
