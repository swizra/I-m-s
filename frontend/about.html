About

Inside KEFE.

KEFE brings together a modern self-care storefront, customer account area, support inbox, delivery tracking, and staff backoffice in one connected experience.

What KEFE includes

Public shop, customer accounts, cart, checkout, admin products, categories, orders, and analytics.

Primary audience

Customers browsing self-care essentials and the teams running store operations behind the scenes.

What KEFE is for

A compact explanation of the project and why these pages exist.

KEFE presents a small but complete inventory and shop experience. The public side focuses on browsing, discovery, and order placement. The protected admin side focuses on updating products, prices, stock levels, categories, and order status.

Careers, customer support, and delivery tracking are tied to the same data model, so store operations and customer-facing behavior stay aligned across the site.

How the experience works

Main user-facing areas of the site.

  • The shop page lets visitors search, filter, sort, open product details, and add items to the cart.
  • Customer access pages let users create an account, sign in, and review previous orders.
  • The admin area lets operators manage categories, products, stock, and order workflows.
  • Dashboard analytics provide quick visual summaries of inventory health and sales activity.

How KEFE operates

A short overview of the site experience.

KEFE is designed as a clear, dependable digital retail experience with emphasis on discovery, inventory accuracy, and responsive customer care. Product launches, account activity, hiring workflows, and support history all connect through the same storefront ecosystem.
