Frequently Asked Questions

Find practical answers about ordering, payment, shipping, returns, and customer accounts before reaching out to the KEFE support team.

Last updated

March 24, 2026

Customer care hours

Monday to Friday, 09:00 to 17:00 CET, excluding public holidays.

Do I need an account to place an order?

KEFE is designed around customer accounts so that orders, delivery status updates, and saved favorites stay accessible after checkout. If you do not have an account yet, the customer access page lets you create one in a few steps.

When will my order ship?

Orders placed before 14:00 CET on business days usually enter processing the same day. Most Swiss deliveries are dispatched within 1 business day once payment and stock are confirmed. During launches, restocks, or high-volume periods, processing can take slightly longer.

Can I change or cancel an order after checkout?

If the order has not entered fulfillment yet, our support team can often update the shipping address, remove an item, or cancel the order. Once an order is packed or handed to the carrier, changes are usually no longer possible.

For fastest help, contact orders@kefe.ch with your order number and the exact change you need.

Where can I see my order history and invoices?

The My Orders page is the main source for order history, current status, and key account details. If an invoice or payment confirmation is missing, support can send a copy after verifying the email address used at checkout.

How do returns work?

Unopened items can generally be returned within 30 days of delivery. Damaged, incorrect, or incomplete orders should be reported within 7 days so the team can review the case and offer a replacement, exchange, or refund where appropriate.

How can I reach support?

General support is available at support@kefe.ch. Order questions are handled at orders@kefe.ch, and privacy, accessibility, and security requests are routed through their dedicated channels listed on the Contact page.
