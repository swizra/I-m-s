Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how KEFE collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal data when you browse the store, create an account, place orders, or use related pages and tools.

Last updated

March 23, 2026

Contact

privacy@kefe.ch

1. Who we are

KEFE operates the storefront, customer account area, and related administrative tools described on this site. For the purposes of applicable data protection law, KEFE acts as the controller of personal data processed through these pages unless a different role is stated for a particular activity.

2. Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy applies to information processed when you visit our website, open product pages, use filters, create a customer account, sign in, place orders, review order history, or otherwise interact with KEFE. It also covers technical event data that may be processed to secure and operate the service.

3. Categories of personal data we may collect

  • Identity data, such as your full name and chosen username.
  • Contact data, such as your email address and shipping or delivery address.
  • Account credentials, such as passwords or authentication-related records.
  • Order data, such as purchased items, quantities, totals, notes, and order timestamps.
  • Technical data, such as browser details, device information, IP-related logs, and page interactions.
  • Session and local storage data needed to keep cart contents and signed-in state functioning.
  • Support or communication data when you contact us about the service.

4. How we collect data

  • Directly from you when you fill in forms, register, sign in, or submit orders.
  • Automatically when your browser loads pages, assets, APIs, and account features.
  • From authenticated actions in the service, such as order placement or profile access.
  • From administrative updates that affect your customer account or order records.

5. Purposes of processing

  • To operate the storefront and display products, categories, prices, and availability.
  • To create and maintain customer accounts.
  • To process cart actions, checkout submissions, and order history access.
  • To secure the platform, detect misuse, troubleshoot errors, and maintain reliability.
  • To administer the service, manage stock, and review activity in the admin environment.
  • To comply with legal obligations, enforce our terms, and document operational events.

6. Legal bases

Depending on the context, we process personal data where necessary to perform a contract with you, to comply with legal obligations, to pursue legitimate interests in operating and securing the service, or on the basis of your consent where consent is required.

7. Authentication and account data

When you create or access an account, we process login identifiers, password information, and session-related data to authenticate you, maintain account access, and protect the integrity of the service. We may also use related event data to investigate suspicious sign-in behavior or unauthorized access attempts.

8. Orders and transactional records

When you place an order, we process the information required to create, store, display, and administer that order. This may include your full name, username, email address, delivery details, ordered items, totals, timestamps, and order status history. These records may also be visible to authorized administrative users for service and operational purposes.

9. Cookies, local storage, and similar technologies

KEFE may use browser storage and similar client-side technologies to keep core features working. For example, local storage may be used to retain cart contents, customer sign-in state, or interface preferences between visits. These technologies are used primarily to support functionality, security, and usability.

10. Analytics, logs, and diagnostics

We may process technical logs and diagnostic information to understand application behavior, monitor service health, troubleshoot issues, and review operational events. This can include timestamps, route access, API usage patterns, browser or device information, and error details associated with requests.

11. How we share data

  • With authorized internal operators and administrators who need access to run the service.
  • With hosting, infrastructure, database, or support providers acting on our instructions.
  • With legal or regulatory authorities where disclosure is required by law or necessary to protect rights.
  • With professional advisers in connection with compliance, security, or legal matters.

12. International data transfers

If personal data is accessed from or stored in countries outside your own, we take steps intended to ensure an appropriate level of protection, which may include contractual safeguards, provider assessments, and organizational controls.

13. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including account operation, order history, security, legal compliance, dispute handling, and technical continuity. Retention periods may vary by data type and applicable obligations.

14. Security

We apply technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and loss. These measures may include access controls, authentication checks, role-based permissions, storage restrictions, and monitoring of service activity. No system, however, can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

15. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have rights to request access to your personal data, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion, object to certain processing, request restriction, request portability, or withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a competent supervisory authority.

16. Children’s privacy

KEFE is not intended to knowingly collect personal data from children in violation of applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal data improperly, please contact us so that we can review the situation and take appropriate action.

17. Third-party content and links

The service may reference external resources, assets, or links. When you follow a link to a third-party site or service, that third party may process data under its own policies. We encourage you to review those policies separately.

18. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the service, our practices, legal requirements, or technical architecture. The latest version will always be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

19. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise a privacy right, please contact us at privacy@kefe.ch.
