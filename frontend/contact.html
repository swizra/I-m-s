Contact KEFE

Reach the right team for order support, account help, accessibility feedback, privacy requests, or general questions about the KEFE storefront.

Business hours

Monday to Friday, 09:00 to 17:00 CET, excluding public holidays

Customer care channels

  • General support: support@kefe.ch
  • Orders and shipping: orders@kefe.ch
  • Privacy and data rights: privacy@kefe.ch
  • Accessibility feedback: accessibility@kefe.ch
  • Security reports: security@kefe.ch
  • Press inquiries: press@kefe.ch

Response times

We aim to answer general customer care and order questions within 1 business day. Privacy, accessibility, security, and press requests are usually acknowledged within 2 business days and may take longer when specialist review is required.

What to include in your message

For faster help, include your order number, the email used during checkout, and a short summary of the issue. Screenshots or exact product names are helpful when the question relates to a catalog page or order item.

Business and media inquiries

For collaboration requests, coursework reviews, or media questions, contact press@kefe.ch and include the organization name, intended use, and requested response timeline.
