KEFE aims to present self-care products with clear descriptions, formulation context, and easy-to-compare information so customers can shop with more confidence.

Goal

Make product pages easier to compare and easier to trust at a glance.

Questions about a product?

Customer care can help clarify item details before checkout.

How products are described

Product pages aim to explain purpose, texture, usage context, and core positioning in plain language so customers can compare items without reading marketing-heavy copy.

Labeling priorities

  • Clear naming and concise summaries.
  • Simple category placement for easier filtering.
  • Descriptions that help customers understand daily use and fit.
  • Key formulation notes where they improve customer decision-making.

How we think about formulation copy

We avoid vague claims and try to keep product language specific. When a formulation is positioned around hydration, comfort, cleansing, or daily ritual use, the copy should help the customer understand that benefit without exaggeration.

Reading product details

When comparing products, use the title, category, short description, availability, and related items together. Those details give the clearest view of how a product fits into the broader KEFE assortment.

Need more information?

If a product page does not answer a specific question about use, fit, or product positioning, contact support before ordering. We would rather clarify a detail than leave a customer guessing at checkout.
