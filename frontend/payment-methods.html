Policies

Payment Methods

Review which payment methods KEFE accepts, how billing checks work, and what to do if a checkout attempt is declined, delayed, or duplicated.

Currency

All prices are shown in CHF unless a different currency is clearly stated at checkout.

Order acceptance

Payment confirmation and stock validation are both required before dispatch.

Accepted methods

KEFE may support major credit cards, debit cards, TWINT, and selected digital wallet options depending on the checkout environment. The payment methods shown during checkout are the currently available options for that session.

Billing checks

  • Orders may be reviewed when billing information appears incomplete or inconsistent.
  • Temporary authorization steps can occur before a payment is fully finalized.
  • Payment failures do not always mean a charge was captured successfully.

Pre-authorizations and duplicate attempts

Some banks or providers may briefly display a pending authorization before the payment is fully settled. If you retry checkout several times, more than one pending authorization can appear even when only one order is eventually created.

If a payment fails

Confirm the billing details, retry only after checking the previous attempt, and contact support if the storefront shows conflicting payment or order information.

Invoices and receipts

Order confirmations and payment records are tied to the customer account used during checkout. If you need a copy of the invoice or payment receipt, support can help after verifying the order details.

Payment support

If you see a failed payment, pending duplicate charge, or incomplete checkout, contact orders@kefe.ch with your order number or the billing email address used during the payment attempt.
